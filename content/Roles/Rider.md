# Rider

Riders are mobile operators who transport currency between locations or deliver/collect cash between branches and clients.

## Responsibilities

- Carry foreign currency or PHP between [[Branches/Branch List|branches]]
- Handle pickup and delivery transactions
- Log transfers in the system

## In the System

- Riders have their own login and device ([[Features/Terminal & Branch Select|Rider Phone terminal]])
- Rider holdings are factored into the [[Features/Dashboard|PHP cash total]] on the dashboard
- Can be involved in [[Features/Replenishment|replenishment]] runs

## Demo User

`ridertest` — fixed password, `is_demo = true`, excluded from [[Features/EOD Reports|EOD reports]]
