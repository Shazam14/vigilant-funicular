# Gold Branch (GOLD)

**Branch code:** `GOLD`
**Address:** Sitio Seabreeze, Pusok, Lapu-Lapu City

## Overview

The Gold branch is in **Sitio Seabreeze, Pusok** — the same barangay as the [[Branches/MAIN|Main branch]], in the coastal strip of Lapu-Lapu City near Mactan Island's beachfront resorts and dive sites. Tourist exchange traffic is significant here.

## Operations

- High tourism-adjacent volume, especially USD, EUR, AUD
- [[Roles/Cashier|Cashier]] + [[Roles/Rider|rider]] operational model
- Close proximity to Main allows quick [[Features/Replenishment|replenishment]] runs
