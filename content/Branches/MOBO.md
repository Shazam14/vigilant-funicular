# Monekat Mobo Branch (MOBO)

**Branch code:** `MOBO`
**Address:** Basdiot, Moalboal, Cebu

## Overview

Monekat Mobo is the **southernmost branch** of Kedco FX, located in Basdiot, Moalboal — a popular diving and tourist destination on the southwest coast of Cebu. It operates under the **Monekat** sub-brand.

## Operations

- Serves dive tourists and OFWs in the Moalboal corridor
- Most geographically remote branch — stock replenishment logistics require advance planning
- [[Roles/Rider|Rider]] trips to/from hub are longer; float management is more critical here
- Same branch_id stamping and [[Features/EOD Reports|EOD reports]] as all other branches

## Terminals

Devices configured via the [[Features/Terminal & Branch Select|branch + terminal selector]]:
- Counter 1, Counter 2, Counter 3
- Rider Phone
- Supervisor Tablet
