# ESY 2 Branch (ESY2)

**Branch code:** `ESY2`
**Address:** ML Quezon National Highway, Pusok, Lapu Lapu City, Cebu

## Overview

ESY 2 shares the same street address as [[Branches/MAIN|Main]] — both are on ML Quezon National Highway in Pusok, Lapu-Lapu City. They operate as **separate branch codes** in the system with independent shifts, floats, and daily positions.

## Operations

- Operates independently of Main despite same street
- Separate `branch_id` ensures transactions, shifts, and EOD reports are never commingled with Main
- [[Roles/Cashier|Cashier]] and float are tracked per-branch

## Terminals

Devices configured via the [[Features/Terminal & Branch Select|branch + terminal selector]]:
- Counter 1, Counter 2, Counter 3
- Rider Phone
- Supervisor Tablet
