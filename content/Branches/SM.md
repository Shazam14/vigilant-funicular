# SM Branch (SM)

**Branch code:** `SM`
**Address:** Gspot Food Park, Kaohsiung St., Mabolo, Cebu City

## Overview

The SM branch is located at **Gspot Food Park** in Mabolo, Cebu City — a food and lifestyle area near the SM commercial district. It captures a mix of retail and tourist traffic.
