# Main Branch (MAIN)

**Branch code:** `MAIN`
**Address:** ML Quezon National Highway, Pusok, Lapu Lapu City, Cebu

## Overview

The Main branch is the **primary hub** of Kedco FX operations. It handles the highest transaction volume and typically serves as the reference point for carry-in rates and capital allocation to other branches.

## Role in Operations

- Largest float allocation among all branches
- [[Roles/Rider|Riders]] typically run from MAIN to smaller branches for [[Features/Replenishment|replenishments]] and foreign currency stock delivery
- [[Roles/Admin|Admin]] operations and [[Features/EOD Reports|EOD reports]] are primarily managed from this branch

## Terminals

Devices are configured via the [[Features/Terminal & Branch Select|branch + terminal selector]]:
- Counter 1, Counter 2, Counter 3
- Rider Phone
- Supervisor Tablet

## Location Note

MAIN and [[Branches/ESY2|ESY2]] share the same street address (ML Quezon National Highway, Pusok) — they are physically adjacent locations under separate branch codes.
