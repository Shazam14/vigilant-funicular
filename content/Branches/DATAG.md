# Monekat Datag Branch (DATAG)

**Branch code:** `DATAG`
**Address:** Maribago, City of Lapu-Lapu, Cebu

## Overview

Monekat Datag is located in **Maribago**, a beachfront barangay in Lapu-Lapu City known for its resorts and dive operators. It operates under the **Monekat** sub-brand of Kedco FX, targeting tourist and OFW-adjacent clientele.

## Operations

- Beachfront resort area drives foreign currency demand (USD, EUR, AUD especially)
- [[Roles/Rider|Rider]] connects with the Main cluster for stock and replenishment runs
- Same system, same [[Features/EOD Reports|EOD report]] flow as all other branches

## Terminals

Devices configured via the [[Features/Terminal & Branch Select|branch + terminal selector]]:
- Counter 1, Counter 2, Counter 3
- Rider Phone
- Supervisor Tablet
