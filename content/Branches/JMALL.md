# Jmall Branch (JMALL)

**Branch code:** `JMALL`
**Address:** V. Albino St. Bakilid, Mandaue City, Cebu

## Overview

The Jmall branch is located in **Bakilid, Mandaue City** — the commercial area surrounding J Centre Mall. It serves the north Cebu corridor and captures spillover from the large mall foot traffic.

## Operations

- Mid-volume retail branch
- [[Roles/Cashier|Cashier]] handles transactions; [[Roles/Admin|admin]] monitors remotely via dashboard
- [[Features/Shift Management|Shifts]] and [[Features/EOD Reports|EOD reports]] follow standard branch flow

## Terminals
