# Bai Branch (BAI)

**Branch code:** `BAI`
**Address:** Bai Hotel, Piano Avenue COR C.D. Seno St., CSSEAZ Mantuyong, Mandaue City

## Overview

The Bai branch is located inside **Bai Hotel** in Mantuyong, Mandaue City. Hotel-adjacent placement means a significant share of clientele are business travelers and hotel guests exchanging foreign currency.

## Operations
