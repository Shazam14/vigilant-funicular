## About Kedco FX

Kedco FX is a multi-branch foreign exchange management system built for real-time currency trading operations. It handles [[Roles/Cashier|cashier]] transactions, [[Roles/Rider|rider]] pickups, [[Features/Dashboard|branch dashboards]], and end-of-day reconciliation across [[Branches/Branch List|9 branches]].
